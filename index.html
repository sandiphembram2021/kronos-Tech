Sandip Hembram is the visionary founder and CEO of Kronos Tech, bringing over 5 years of experience in system programming and software architecture. His passion for low-level programming and developer tools led him to create Kronos OS, a complete operating system built from scratch.

Under his leadership, Kronos Tech has evolved from an open-source OS project into a comprehensive developer services company, serving hundreds of developers worldwide with enterprise-grade APIs and cloud infrastructure solutions.

Sandip's technical expertise spans from kernel development to modern web APIs, making him uniquely qualified to understand and solve the complex challenges developers face in today's technology landscape.
